## Theoretical Bandwidth Requirements

### HD720 @ 60 FPS (1280×720)
- Resolution: 1280 × 720 = 921,600 pixels
- Stereo cameras: 2 × 921,600 = 1,843,200 pixels per frame
- Bits per pixel: 16 (YUV422) or 24 (RGB)
- Frame rate: 60 FPS

**Uncompressed bandwidth (worst case):**
- 1,843,200 pixels × 3 bytes × 60 FPS = 332 MB/s

**With lossless compression (~40% ratio typical for ZED):**
- 332 MB/s × 0.4 = ~133 MB/s

**Storage capacity: 248 MB/s available**
**Required: 133 MB/s**
**Headroom: 115 MB/s (87% overhead)**

**STORAGE IS NOT THE BOTTLENECK**
